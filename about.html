About CineWorld

Your trusted place for movie information across all major Indian languages.

Who We Are

CineWorld is a multi-language movie information platform designed for movie lovers across India. Whether you're a fan of Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, or Malayalam cinema, CineWorld brings all movie details to one place.


What We Provide

  • Movie details including cast, director, genre, and descriptions.
  • Streaming platform availability (Amazon Prime, Netflix, Hotstar, etc.).
  • Official trailer links.
  • Clean, responsive design suitable for all devices.
  • Dedicated sections for each movie industry.

Our Goal

To create a simple, beautiful, and complete platform where users can explore and discover movies across different languages without switching between multiple websites.
